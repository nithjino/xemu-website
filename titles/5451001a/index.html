compat_comments: I managed to play up until the Jellyfish Fields Boss - King Jellyfish. Here are my notes:

Notes:
- Cutscene audio stutters and sometimes doesn't match up with the animation.
- Throughout the game, the screen shows an alternating yellow line pattern over the game.
- The In-game cutscenes, menu, and gameplay runs great with little to no lag, depending on how many enemies are on screen.
- There are 2 alternating Assertion failed messages that occur whenever Spongebob does a bubble move (Expression: pg->color_binding || pg->zeta_binding - Line: 2485  & Expression: glGetError() == GL_NO_ERROR - Line: 752).
- Note: In order to bypass these messages and continue playing, you have to ignore these messages around 25-50 times. The most I had to ignore was over 70 times. I'd say stick with Spongebob's standard attack until this problem gets fixed, unless you have to press an important button to progress.
- Fortunately, when you get Patrick, none of his attacks cause an Assertion failed message to pop up.
